Volunteer

Thank you for your interest in the Shawnee Christian Healthcare Center and your desire to partner with us as we seek to minister to the Shawnee neighborhood in the name of Jesus. We have several areas in which you will have an opportunity to serve your neighbor.

Facilities/Maintenance Volunteer

Assist with the internal and external maintenance of the Center to insure cleanliness and order.

Office/Clerical Volunteer

Assist Office Manager in clerical responsibilities including answering telephones and patient registration.

Prayer/Pastoral Care Volunteer

Spiritual intervention including the ability to pray with patients (with informed consent) and discuss issues of faith as they relate to health concerns. Serve as a member of a pastoral care team and health support staff.

Community/Neighborhood Transformation Volunteer

Serve in partnership with Neighborhood Transformation Director in the facilitation and coordination of neighborhood-based initiatives designed to empower residents to self-sufficiency in skills and resources and in their understanding of the redemptive power of Jesus Christ.

Medical Volunteer

Physicians, nurses, and other health professionals who hold a current license will have an opportunity to care for the physicals needs of the patient as well as the spiritual, social and emotional needs by promoting quality and affordable care for those who have limited access to healthcare.

Medical Receptionist

Hours

Monday 10:30am-7pm

Tuesday-Friday 8:30am-5pm

Duties and Responsiblities

  • Greet patients and visitors in prompt and courteous fashion.
  • Collect and update patient demographic and insurance information and make updates in the electronic health record.
  • Collect insurance, co-payments, and balances from patients.
  • Maintain patient confidentiality.
  • Participate as strong team memeber with front office staff.
  • Perform related work as required.
  • Schedule appointments and checking patients out.

Requirements

  • High School Diploma
  • Knowledge of business office procedures, ability to speak clearly and conscisely, ability to read and understand written instructions.
  • Ablilty to maintain effective working relationships with co-workers, patients, and the public.
  • Have at least one year of work experience. Preferably in a medical office setting.
  • Adhere to the Shawnee Christian Healtcare Center Statement of Faith.

If interested in any of the following positions, please submit your resume to our office manager Michele Crider at michele.crider@shawneehealthcare.org.
